### What it is

Swiss One Lab designs **coherent, secure, automation-first platform environments** for professional operations.

- Secure, multi-environment platform architecture
- Automation, observability &amp; governance built in by design
- AI-assisted operations — ship faster, more reliably
- **Local-first &amp; sovereign** — software that keeps data under its owner's control

---

### What we build

| Domain | Focus |

### Principles

- **Security-first** — hardening and governance built in, not bolted on
- **Sovereign by design** — local-first, offline-capable; you own your data
- **Modular** — composable tools over monolithic lock-in
- **AI-assisted** — local models and agents augment the craft

---

### Sovereignty &amp; Trust

- **Sovereign software** — every product is local-first and offline-capable; your data stays on your own infrastructure, never locked into a third-party cloud.
- **Owned by Swiss One Lab** — all products are proprietary works of Swiss One Lab, designed, signed, and maintained in-house.
- **Digitally signed** — each release carries a **SealProof** digital signature attesting authorship and integrity (`SOL-<year>-<hash>` fingerprint derived from the source).
- **Defined terms** — licensing, usage, and access conditions are specified per product; operational access is reserved to approved users and controlled environments.
- **Swiss-grounded** — engineered in Geneva, Switzerland, to Swiss standards of privacy, reliability, and discretion.
